Our Services at Mosse

Compassion. Dignity. Detail. At Mosse Home Care, every service is designed to deliver premium care with integrity -- tailored for adults aged 55 to 65 in a private home setting.

Levels of Care

Basic Assisted Living

Foundational support with meals, safety, and daily routine. Ideal for those who benefit from light supervision in a structured, nurturing home setting.

  • Daily meals & snacks
  • Medication reminders
  • Housekeeping & laundry
  • Safety checks & 24/7 staffing

Cognitive & Mental Health Support

Tailored care for residents with mental health and or trauma-based diagnoses. we blend emotional support with clinical coordination.

  • De-escalation & redirection support
  • Behavioral care planning
  • 1:1 emotional check-ins
  • Collaborative mental health coordination

Complex Care Needs

Higher-level support for residents requiring clinical monitoring, delegated nursing tasks, or close coordination with medical providers.

  • Insulin injections (RN delegated)
  • Seizure, fall, or diabetic protocols
  • Delegated clinical observations
  • Special diets, mobility & compression assist
