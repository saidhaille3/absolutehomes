Frequently Asked Questions

General Facility Questions

1. What age group do you serve?

We specialize in serving adults aged 55–65. Our home is structured and licensed specifically for this age group under MDH regulations.

2. Are you licensed by the Minnesota Department of Health (MDH)?

Yes. Mosse Home Care LLC is a licensed Basic Assisted Living facility under the Minnesota Department of Health.

3. What is your staff-to-resident ratio?

We maintain a 1:1 staff-to-resident ratio during waking hours to ensure individualized attention and timely support.

4. How many residents do you accept?

We are licensed for a maximum of 5 residents to maintain a quiet, personalized home setting.

5. What types of rooms are available?

We offer private bedrooms with options for custom furnishing, personal décor, and safety features like grab bars and emergency access.

6. Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately, we do not allow personal pets in the home.

7. Can I schedule a tour?

Yes! Please use the "Book Tour" button or contact us directly at (612) 636‑4172 to arrange a private walkthrough of the home.

8. What’s included in your base rate?

Our base rate typically includes housing, meals, housekeeping, laundry, 24/7 staff availability, medication reminders, and delegated clinical oversight. Specific services are outlined in the client’s Service Agreement.

9. What makes you different from larger facilities or group homes?

We are a real residential home. We provide warmth, cultural understanding, 1:1 care, and compliance-driven excellence without the impersonal feel of large institutions.
