Our Story

Mosse Home Care was born out of a real family caregiving journey. After witnessing the gaps in large institutional care, our founders envisioned a more personal, compliant, and comforting home alternative, one grounded in cultural respect and MDH excellence.

Licensed in 2025 and located in Rosemount, Minnesota, Mosse proudly provides a home-like setting for aging adults who deserve individualized care.

From handwritten protocols to formal survey compliance, we’ve built Mosse brick by brick, and we’re proud to call it home.

What Makes Us Different

Real Home Setting

Our 5-bed licensed residence offers the intimacy and warmth of a true family home, tailored for peace and privacy.

Exclusive Care Ratio

With a 1:1 or 1:2 staffing model, our team provides an elite level of personalized attention that larger facilities simply cannot match.

Behavioral Expertise

Specialized support for anxiety and complex behavioral needs, managed with de-escalation expertise and clinical precision.

Culinary Excellence

Nutritious, culturally-inspired meals prepared fresh daily. We believe dining is a dignifying experience to be celebrated.

RN-Delegated Care

Rigorous clinical oversight following MDH 144G standards, ensuring medication and wellness are managed with absolute integrity.

Cultural Respect

We honor the unique faith, language, and heritage of our residents, weaving cultural sensitivity into the fabric of daily life.
